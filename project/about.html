Perfume Making Classes

I had strarted these classes because I had always loved perfume. I wanted the opportunity to create a space where people can learn the art of making a perfume and be able to connect with other people.

With Spritz we create a fruitful learning and community building experince.



Our customers at Spritz often feel refreshed and at peace after our classes. They go home having learnt something new and having met new people. They also get to take home the perfume that they created, which is personal to them and serves as something to remeber the experince by.
